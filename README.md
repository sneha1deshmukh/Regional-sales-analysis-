# Regional Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
This project focuses on analyzing **regional sales performance across the U.S.** using historical sales data. The dashboard provides actionable insights into **high-performing products, profitable channels, top states, and seasonal trends**. It helps management make **data-driven decisions** for pricing, product strategy, promotions, and regional expansion.

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Total Revenue:** $1.2B  
- **Total Profit:** $461.8M  
- **Profit Margin:** 37.36%  
- **Total Orders:** 64K  
- **Revenue per Order:** $19.3K  

---

## Charts & Visualizations
- **Line Charts:** Monthly Revenue Trend, Monthly Profit Trend  
- **Histogram:** Order Value Distribution  
- **Scatter Plots:** Unit Price vs Profit Margin, Revenue vs Profitability  
- **Bar Charts:** Top Products, Top Customers, Best States, High-Margin Products  
- **Donut/Pie Charts:** Revenue by Channel, Profit by Channel, Margin by Route  
- **Map Visual:** Total Profit by State  
- **KPI Cards:** Quick insights at a glance  
- **Category Comparison Charts:** Revenue and Profit Margin by Region  

---

## Dimensions Used
- **Time:** Month  
- **Products:** Product Name, Product ID  
- **Channels:** Wholesale, Distributor, Export  
- **Geography:** State, Region (Midwest, Northeast, South, West)  
- **Customer:** Top/Bottom Customers (Company Names)  
- **Price Bands & Order Value Bins:** For distribution and trend analysis

## Key Insights
- **California** contributes the highest revenue ($228.8M), making up **19.5%** of total U.S. sales.  
- **West region** consistently shows the highest profit margin (~37.5%).  
- **Product 26** is the top revenue driver, generating $117M.  
- High-margin products maintain **margin bands around 38–40%**, indicating strong pricing efficiency.  
- **Wholesale** drives the majority of revenue (~54%), followed by Distributor and Export channels.  
- **Order value distribution is right-skewed** — most customers place smaller orders; high-value orders are rare but impactful.  
- **Monthly revenue and profit trends show seasonal peaks**, particularly in March, June, and October.  

---

## Purpose of the Dashboard
1. Analyze regional sales performance across the U.S.  
2. Identify high-performing products, channels, and states.  
3. Understand seasonality, demand patterns, and profitability trends.  
4. Provide management with a consolidated view of sales growth drivers, customer behavior, and channel efficiency.  
5. Support **data-driven decisions** for pricing, product strategy, promotional targets, and regional expansion.
